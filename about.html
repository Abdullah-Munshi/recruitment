About Us

What We Do

Network Recruitment Wales is dedicated to delivering a high-quality workforce solution to our clients. We prioritise innovation and customer service in all that we do. Our speciality lies in providing cost-effective support services that offer the best possible temporary staff, backed by exceptional customer service that's accessible every hour of every day, all year round.

Our Purpose

The purpose of the Network Recruitment Wales Group is

“To find our candidates great job roles and to find our clients great people”

We achieve our purpose by
implementing our threefold mission:

1

Helping the healthcare and education sector provide appropriately qualified and skilled temporary workers, as and when required to meet the fluctuating requirements for the safe and professional delivery of their services. And provide a value for money service.

2

Enabling registered healthcare and education workers to use their skills by having the opportunity of family friendly, flexible working arrangements, suitable remuneration and professional support and ongoing training and development.

3

Developing the business based on efficient and effective systems and sound business planning so that the outcome is positive for all service users, temporary workers and the Network Recruitment Wales group and its brands.

Our Values

Caring

At Network Recruitment Wales we treat people how we would want to be treated, with dignity and respect. We are always polite, honest and friendly. We are here to help and we always endeavour to make all of our service users feel valued.

Professional

Network Recruitment Wales has a very experienced, loyal team of office staff. The team comprises of recruitment experts, experienced resourcing, compliance and placement coordinators, qualified nurses, teachers and social workers. Their vast knowledge, enthusiasm and understanding means, they are able to manage the most difficult of situations with sensitivity and appropriate support.

Reliable

At Network Recruitment Wales we are fully committed and dedicated to all our services users. We do what we say we are going to do. We focus on what matters most. We do not put off making difficult decisions if they are the right decisions. If we cannot do something we will always explain why and if appropriate sign-post you to alternative services and/or organisations.
