Why Choose Us

As a candidate, consider the exceptional advantages of choosing SET Recruitment, an agency proudly associated with the Welsh Government Framework. Their commitment to excellence is your assurance of a reliable and top-tier recruitment process.

­­Candidates As well as SET Recruitment being 100% free to join, we also offer all the below benefits:

  • A weekly prize draw of £100 for every member of staff (1 shift worked = 1 entry)
  • Sector leading contract rates
  • Free mandatory training courses (when applicable)
  • Free Enhanced DBS Checks
  • A committed candidate care team to support you every step of the way
  • We also offer a refer-a-friend scheme where you can earn up to £250!
