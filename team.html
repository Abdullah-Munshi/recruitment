Meet The Team

Our Ethos

Established in 2000, MPS Healthcare has built a successful business through our unique service offering. We provide a quality workforce solution to our clients by putting patient care, innovation and customer service at the heart of everything we do. MPS Healthcare is one of Wales’ largest and most respected nursing agency and the leading supplier on the NHS All Wales Agency Framework, where MPS supply over 50% of nursing professionals across Wales.
